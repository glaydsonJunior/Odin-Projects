Ravioli

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 pound sausage
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar marinara sauce, such as Rao's®
  • 1 (25 ounce) bag frozen ravioli
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 (8-ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • ⅓ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.

Step 2

Heat a skillet over medium high heat. Cook and stir sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, breaking up large pieces with a wooden spoon, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain fat from the skillet.

Step 3

Pour marinara sauce into the skillet, and stir to combine with sausage. Turn off heat.

Step 4

Stir cottage cheese, softened cream cheese, yogurt, Parmesan cheese, parsley, and granulated garlic together in a large bowl. Add cheese ravioli to the bowl and toss together until all ravioli are coated with cheese mixture.

Step 5

Spread the ravioli mixture evenly in the baking dish. Pour meat sauce over ravioli and spread evenly. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly on top.

Step 6

Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is golden brown, about 45 minutes. Remove from the oven; rest about 10 minutes before serving. Sprinkle with thinly sliced basil.
